Simple House

Inspired by Call Of Duty: Warzone urban Buildings.

Project Overview

This project showcases the development of an House, i call it House_01 because this is my first Completed Model. i used vertex color to use it in Substance Painter and the Material used in the Outer Wall is custom made in Substance Designer. Unreal engine is to showcase the House.

Technical Details

  • Custom Material for house wall
  • High poly House Model
  • High Resolution screenshot
  • Advance Modeling technics

Tools Used

  • Unreal Engine
  • Maya
  • Substance Painter
